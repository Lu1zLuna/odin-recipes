Cajun Potato Wedges

Table of contents

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Description

This vegan oatmeal smoothie has a deep pink color and a rich, creamy texture. It's very filling, and perfect for people in a rush in the morning. You don't have to give up a good breakfast when it's this fast to make! I use vitamin fortified soy milk.

Ingredients

  • 4 baking potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ teaspoons onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.
  2. Cut each potato into 12 wedges; place in a large bowl. Toss potatoes with olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, onion powder, and paprika. Arrange in a single layer on prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes. Turn potatoes over and continue baking until golden and crisp, 25 to 30 minutes longer.
